Justice Center Navigator

Designed to assist defendants and visitors at the Criminal Justice Center in Downtown Houston, this self-service kiosk enhances navigation and access to court information. The system runs on a Raspberry Pi 4 with a 7” touchscreen and is housed in a custom 49” wooden pedestal for visibility and ease of use.

The Pi launches a web application in kiosk mode, displaying building directories, Misdemeanor/Felony court schedules, and real-time defendant docket information from a cloud database. The application is built with HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, and MongoDB.

Hands-free Drone tracking & recognition

Voice-Controlled Drone for Tracking & Recognition (Senior Project) Developed a voice-controlled drone that integrates face tracking to enhance standard drone capabilities. The system enables voice commands for launching and landing while using the onboard camera to detect and track human faces.

As Project Manager, I ensured deadlines and requirements were met, facilitated team communication, resolved conflicts, and contributed to Python module development. The project is Linux-based, leveraging ROS (Robot Operating System), OpenCV, and the Google Voice API.
