Justice Center Navigator

jan 2023 - may 2023

Association: Harris County, university of houston-clear lake: capstone

Technologies Used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, MongoDB, Raspberry Pi

Role: lead Developer

Designed to assist defendants and visitors at the Criminal Justice Center in Downtown Houston, this self-service kiosk enhances navigation and access to court information. The system runs on a Raspberry Pi 4 with a 7” touchscreen and is housed in a custom 49” wooden pedestal for visibility and ease of use. The Pi launches a web application in kiosk mode, displaying building directories, Misdemeanor/Felony court schedules, and real-time defendant docket information from a cloud database.
